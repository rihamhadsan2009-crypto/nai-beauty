Founder note

NAI Beauty was created to celebrate Arab women and their modern identity. The brand was born from a simple belief, beauty should feel effortless, luxurious, and honest. Every product is designed to enhance your natural look, not hide it. Science guides our formulas, culture inspires our soul, and your everyday life shapes our vision.

Why clean beauty

Clean beauty means choosing formulas that respect your skin and your health, avoiding unnecessary harsh ingredients, focusing on high-performance actives, and prioritizing transparency. Clean does not mean boring, it means smarter, safer, and luxurious in a modern way.

About the first product

This lip oil is designed for Arab lips, it nourishes deeply, adds shine without stickiness, and gives a natural healthy color. The formula is lightweight, comfortable, and created to fit everyday life from morning coffee to late evenings.
